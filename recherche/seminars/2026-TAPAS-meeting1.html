 TAPAS-ANR meeting - Nice, 7-8 Janvier 2026

Hierarchical clock theory

  • Developing an Event-B theory to embed the TESL language into Event-B models
    • developing a formal model of the semantics of TESL operators.
  • TESL time model
    • Time is represented through clocks, where each clock models the occurrence of events.
    • Event-B event executions are defined as ticks of the corresponding clocks

Hierarchical clock connector

  • Integration of the TESL solver with the ProB animator and model checker
  • The TESL solver generates execution scenarios that drive the animation of Event-B models
  • Time-guided validation of Event-B models based on a formal clock-based time model.
